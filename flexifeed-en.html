VERSATILE

Reliable, quiet, efficient, smart.

EFlexifeed is a feeder capable of managing entire product families without necessity of mechanical interventions. Thanks to electronics, digital programming and image recognition systems, Flexifeed can manage quickly and at no cost any production change.

RELIABLE

FlexiFeed is capable of feeding components uniformly, with continuity and increased efficiency. It is a solution capable of outlining defective parts before they enter the production process. Moreover, cloud connectivity allows to check remotely performances and intervene predictively reducing troubleshoots and down-times.

PERFORMING

Flexifeed is a high performing solution, capable of feeding at a speed between 60 and 120 PPM, being at the same time flexible, precise and reprogrammable. The control system optimizes speed, acceleration and frequency parameters based on shapes and geometry of the components. Flexifeed is available in different shapes and dimensions and it is customizable according to customer needs. Moreover, thanks to the drastic reduction of troubleshoots, faults, dead time cycles and jams, Flexifeed allows to substantially optimize overall equipment efficiency. Flexifeed is a solution that can easily be interconnected with other application systems, and is managed through a unique software solution if installed on a Flexim automation solution, in line with Industry 4.0 principles.
